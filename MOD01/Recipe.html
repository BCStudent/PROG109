Week #1

Carrot Fries

Created by Paul Walters

January 12, 2018

Fries made from Carrots in only 20 minutes?

Ingredients
  • 2 pounds large carrots, trimmed and peeled
  • 6 Tbsp. white rice flour
  • 6 Tbsp. canola oil
  • 2 tsp. garam masala (an Indian spice blend that can include cinnamon, pepper, cumin and cloves)
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 4500 and place racks on the 2nd and 3rd racks
  2. Cut carrots into French-fry-size sticks, about 3" long
  3. In a large bowl, toss carrots with rice flour to coat and shake off excess flour.
  4. Spread the carrots without crowding, in a single layer on two rimmed baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake 7 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, combine oil, garam masala, and salt in a large bowl
  6. Gently remove carrots from the baking sheets and toss with oil mixture
  7. Return carrots to baking sheet and bake approx. 10-15 minutes or until crisp-tender and lightly browned along the edges
