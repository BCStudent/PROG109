Winter2018-Q1

My first quarter back to school in seven years proved to be very exciting!

  • PROG 109 Introduction to Web Development
    • Instructor: Maricel Medina-Mora, MSc. MEd
    • Credits: 5
    • During the past 3 months, I've gone from having no Web Development experience to knowing how to "look under the hood" to see how a site was built! Personally, I found JavaScript to be very challenging and realize it will take more than a quarter to understand it.

  • BUSIT 103 SQL Fundamentals
    • Instructor: Lisa Harris
    • Credits: 5
    • Another class I had no experience with going in and feel as if I barely scratched the surface! Initially, I was very confident and able to keep up with lessons until we began using sub-queries within sub-queries within sub-queries... Now I know why SQL skills are in demand and fetch high wages!

  • Math 093 Algebra Review
    • Instructor: Mausumi Maulik
    • Credits: 1-5
    • This was a "self-study" course to help me prepare for MATH 099 and beyond. I found the work to be very challenging and hope I remember everything I learned when I take the final on March 21st.
